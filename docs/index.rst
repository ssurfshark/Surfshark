How to Log In to Your Surfshark Account
=======================================

Follow these simple steps to sign in:

* Open the Surfshark application or website.
* Select **Log In**.
* Enter your registered email address.
* Type your account password.
* Select **Log In** again.
* Complete verification if required.
* Access your Surfshark dashboard.

Common Login Issues
===================

You may experience login problems due to:

* Incorrect email address.
* Incorrect password.
* Slow internet connection.
* Browser cache issues.
* Outdated application.
* Temporary server maintenance.
* Account verification requests.

How to Fix Login Problems
=========================

If you're unable to log in:

* Verify your email address and password.
* Refresh the login page.
* Clear browser cache and cookies.
* Update the Surfshark application.
* Restart your internet connection.
* Try another supported browser.
* Reset your password.
* Try again after maintenance is complete.
